Council §3.1 rework of the Wave-1 SG-IVTR-AUTONOMY ADR-079 stub (`adr-079-ac-stable-ids`). Replaces the dual-ID system (positional + content-hash) with a single canonical UUIDv4 generated at AC creation, plus a positional alias (AC1/AC2/...) used strictly for display. Defines `_history` retention contract for drift detection and pins the basic `satisfies:<task-id>#<ac-id>` binding shape that T10493 will finalise. Foundation ADR for SAGA T10377 SG-IVTR-AC-BINDING — every other ADR in the saga consumes its AC ID format.
