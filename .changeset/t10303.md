Wires automatic detection + recovery into the brain.db open chokepoint (`packages/core/src/store/memory-sqlite.ts:getBrainDb`). Catches `ERR_SQLITE_ERROR errcode=11` on open AND any failed `PRAGMA quick_check` after open. On detection: quarantines the corrupt DB plus `-wal`/`-shm` sidecars to `<projectRoot>/.cleo/quarantine/brain-malformed-<iso>/`, selects the freshest validated snapshot from `.cleo/backups/snapshot/brain.db.snapshot-*` (or VACUUM-INTO snapshots, or legacy `brain.db.PRE-DUP-FIX-*`), copies it into place, runs a final integrity probe, and re-attempts the open. Emits a single structured Pino warning naming the snapshot timestamp and human-readable data-loss window. Saga: T10281; Epic: T10286.
