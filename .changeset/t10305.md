feat(T10305): db-inventory SSoT artifact + contracts type

Adds the canonical machine-readable inventory of every CLEO SQLite database
at `packages/core/src/store/db-inventory.json` and exposes a typed contract
`DB_INVENTORY: readonly DbInventoryEntry[]` from `@cleocode/contracts`. The
inventory enumerates the 9 ADR-068 charter rows PLUS three audit-discovered
rows (`global-brain`, `global-tasks`, `skills`) from research note
`sg-brain-db-resilience-deep-audit-2026-05-23` — 12 entries total.

Downstream consumers (T10307 fleet survey, T10310 pragma drift, T10311
migration coverage, T10312 doctor integrity, T10320 cross-DB invariants)
iterate `DB_INVENTORY` instead of duplicating the role list. A vitest suite
in `packages/contracts/src/__tests__/db-inventory.test.ts` asserts unique
roles, valid tier literals, canonical path tokens, and that every non-null
`drizzleSchemaPath` + `migrationsDir` resolves to a real file/directory.
